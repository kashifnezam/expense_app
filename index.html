Topic 1: Physical Geography (PYQ)

Summary: BPSC focuses heavily on Bihar's geological structure. Northern Bihar is dominated by Terai/Someshwar hills (Tertiary), while Southern Bihar features Dharwad (oldest) and Vindhyan (Rohtas/Kaimur) systems. Bihar is strictly a "Landlocked State".

Topic 2: River Systems (PYQ)

Summary: Ganga flows 445km in Bihar, entering at Chausa. Kosi (Sorrow of Bihar) and Gandak are key Himalayan rivers. Son and Punpun are key plateau rivers. Oxbow lakes (Kanwar Lake) are vital for Bihar's ecosystem.
